Rules of the Game

Here are four of the coolest dogs around. If you click on one of those doggos, they will give a specific amount of biscuits to you.


If you match Your Biscuits to the Goal Number of Biscuits, you win a Bone. If you get greedy and take too many biscuits, you lose.


I won't tell you how many biscuits each dog has so pay attention to Your Biscuits when you click.
